Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

Punta cana is the perfect beachy stay for any group of students looking for low cost options
We recommend a stay at the whala!urban punta cana hotel. Rooms are as low as 66$ a night and the hotel features a buffet style breakfast, pool, air conditioning, wifi, parking, and gym all while being beachside!
When you're not relaxing by the warm Caribbean seas, enjoying live local entertainment or shopping, we recommend exploring the island on a guided dune buggy tour for as low as 40$. Enjoy a wide range of dining options, including authentic dominican cuisine.
